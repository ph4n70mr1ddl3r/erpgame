How Delegation Works

  • When a category is delegated, events are auto-resolved each quarter
  • The relevant executive picks the best choice based on their skill (skill/100 chance)
  • If no executive is hired for a delegated category, a random choice is made
  • Undelegated events appear in the Decisions page for you to choose manually
