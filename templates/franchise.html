How Franchising Works

  • Franchise Fee: One-time upfront payment when you convert a store to a franchise.
  • Royalties: Ongoing quarterly payments based on a percentage of store revenue.
  • Less Control: Franchised stores generate passive income but you have less operational control.
  • Buy Back: Purchase the franchise back at 70% of the franchise fee to regain full control.
  • Requirement: You must keep at least one company-owned store.
